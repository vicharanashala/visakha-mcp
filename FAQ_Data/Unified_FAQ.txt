1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Q1.1: What is the Full Stack Development Bootcamp about?
The bootcamp introduces learners to full-stack web development concepts, focusing on both frontend and backend technologies using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js). It is designed to help participants understand how complete digital solutions are built.

Q1.2: What is the NPTEL Winter Internship?
This winter internship is hosted by Indian Institute of Technology-Ropar in online mode. It provides hands-on experience in full-stack development with structured mentorship and project work.

Q1.3: Is the bootcamp free?
Yes, the bootcamp is designed to provide learning opportunities without any fees.

5. ViBe PLATFORM - ACCESS & TECHNICAL ISSUES
================================================================================

Q5.1: How do I log in to the ViBe platform?
Step 1: Use the invite link sent to your registered email. You will receive an email from vicharanshala.
Step 2: Open the mail and click on the Accept Invite button in the mail itself.
Step 3: You will be redirected to the login page.
Step 4: Click on Continue as Student, always login as a Student.
Step 5: As soon as you click on Continue as Student you will be redirected to the next page.
Step 6: Now click on Continue with Google so continue with your registered email ID (The one from which you got the link)
Step 7: As soon as you continue as Google you will be redirected to the Dashboard.

Q5.2: I accepted the invite, but it shows "No course enrolled." What should I do?
Step 1: Go to the profile section of your dashboard.
Step 2: Please make sure you're signed in with the same email ID you used during registration. You can verify this by logging into your Google account (the one where you originally received the registration link).
Step 3: If your institute uses an alias email, try logging in with the registered ID directly.
Step 4: If the issue persists, inform support so we can manually add you.

Q5.3: Videos are not playing / stuck on repeat.
Step 1: Make sure you are watching the videos in sequential order without skipping.
Step 2: Allow camera and microphone access (mandatory for progress tracking).
Step 3: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then refresh with Ctrl + Shift + R.

Q5.4: I am getting an "Access Restricted" error.
This usually happens if a step is skipped.
Step 1: Please watch each video in order.
Step 2: If it still persists, try refreshing the page by pressing Ctrl + R. You may be redirected to the dashboard, but don't worry—you can easily return to your course and continue from where you left off.

Q5.5: My quiz answers are correct, but the system is marking them wrong.
Step 1: Please note the video number and case study name
Step 2: Report it to the support desk.
Step 3: In some cases, the issue is resolved from the backend within a few hours.

Q5.6: My dashboard shows less than 100% even after completing all videos/quizzes.
1. Sometimes, progress may take time to update.
2. Refresh your browser and check again.
3. In case of discrepancies, the Google Sheet records maintained by organizers are considered final.

Q5.7: I cannot find my course on the dashboard.
Step 1: Accept the invite link from your registered email.
Step 2: If you already accepted, check under Invites (top-right of dashboard).
Step 3: Accept the course invite by clicking on the "Invites" button located at the top-right corner of the page.
Step 4: If you're still unable to view your courses on the dashboard, please reach out to the Support Desk for assistance.

Q5.8: ViBe is not detecting my face, what should I do?
To ensure your face is properly detected by the software, please follow these guidelines:
1. Sit in a well-lit room.
2. Keep your face uncovered at all times.
3. Ensure the background is clear and not blurred.
4. Position the camera to capture your entire face.
5. Avoid lighting that causes glare or shadow on your face.
6. Confirm that your camera is switched on and has the necessary permissions.
7. Sit in a quiet place to avoid disturbances.
8. Ensure no one else is visible in the camera frame.

Q5.9: Why can't I log in or access the ViBe?
Step 1: Ensure that you have accepted the link sent to your registered email ID.
Step 2: If it still doesn't work then refresh the page by pressing Ctrl + R or clear the cache by pressing Ctrl + Shift + R.
Step 3: If the issue persists, please contact our support desk via email or message, providing your name, registered email ID, and either a screenshot or a screen recording of the problem.

Q5.10: Why do I see the "Quiz Failed" option every time I submit my answers?
This issue can be faced due to several reasons you can do the following steps to overcome it:
1. Refresh the page using Ctrl+R.
2. You can logout and login again.
3. If even after then you are facing the same issue then please contact our support desk via email or message, providing your name, registered email ID, and either a screenshot or a screen recording of the problem.

Q5.11: Why do my videos auto-restart?
The videos can auto restart due to the following reasons:
1. Face not being detected.
2. Blur background
3. Background noise being detected.
4. Face is not visible.
5. More than one person in the camera is being detected.

Q5.12: Can we use Mobile or tablet to access the program?
The software cannot be accessed using a mobile phone or tablet. Please ensure you are using a desktop, PC, or laptop with an active camera and microphone.

Q5.13: What is the last date for completion of all ViBe activity?
All ViBe-related activities and assignments to be completed by a deadline which will be mentioned in your welcome mail.

Q5.14: Is there a tutorial available for the platform?
Yes, the bootcamp tutorial is available in the ViBe homepage and in youtube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ytNdYlK-BU

9. SUPPORT & ISSUE RESOLUTION
================================================================================

Q9.1: Where do I raise my concern initially?
Please send an email to internship-support@vicharanashala.zohodesk.in.
Our support team will respond within 2 hours of your submission (9 AM to 9 PM). Any question submitted beyond 9 PM will be answered the next day.

Q9.2: What if I don't receive a response in 24 hours?
If you do not receive a response within 24 hours, you may raise a second ticket.
In the subject line, mention: "Attention Please – Follow-up on Ticket #[Original Ticket Number]"
This ticket will be escalated to our Mentors Team, who will reach out to you for a voice call discussion and try to resolve the issue directly.

Q9.3: Who will respond to my second ticket?
The Mentors Team will handle the second ticket and schedule a voice call to ensure your issue is clearly understood and resolved.

Q9.4: If my concern still isn't addressed, how do I escalate further?
After 48 hours of your first ticket (and after raising a follow-up), you can escalate the matter via email to the Internship Sponsor, with CC to Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar directly with the following recipients in CC:
- Your department's faculty reference
- Your department HOD
- The DLED Team at dled@iitrpr.ac.in

Ensure you include:
- Your full name and department
- Reference numbers and dates of the two Zoho Desk tickets
- A brief explanation of the unresolved issue

Q9.5: Why do I need to include my faculty reference and HOD in the final escalation email?
This ensures transparency and allows your academic mentors to support you while helping us maintain accountability and timely resolution.

Q9.6: Is this process mandatory for all issues?
Yes, to maintain consistency and ensure efficient support, we request all interns to follow this three-step resolution path.

12. TERMINATION & REJOINING POLICY
================================================================================

Q12.1: I received a termination email due to overlapping academic commitments, but my exams are over now. Can I rejoin the current internship batch?
No. The internship is a full-time commitment, and any academic overlap, even if temporary, disqualifies a participant from continuing. You will need to apply again for the next available internship batch if interested.

Q12.2: Can I appeal my termination if I have valid reasons for missing assignments or sessions?
The internship policy allows no leniency. If you have already received an official termination notice due to either lack of attendance, late submissions, or academic overlap, your internship cannot be reinstated. The rules are strict to maintain fairness and program quality.

Q12.3: I plan to rejoin the internship later. Will it be the same program?
If you wish to participate again, you must reapply and go through the selection process for the next batch. Please note that future internships may not be funded by any organization or IIT Ropar.
